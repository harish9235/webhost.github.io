Q.1)
A person age is 2/5 of his age of his mother after 8 years he will be one half of age of his mother. How old is mother at present
  1. 30 Years
  2. 40 Years
  3. 27 Years
  4. 42 Years
Answer :Option B

Present age of person and his mother in the ratio of 2/5 And, after 8 years their age ratio is 1/2 So, (2x+8)/(5x+8)=1/2

4X+16=5X+8

X=8

Therefore, present age of mother is 8*5=40 Years

Q.2)
Sachin is 7 Years younger than Rahul and their ages are in the ratio 7:9, What is age of Sachin
  1. 32 Years
  2. 40 Years
  3. 24.5 Years
  4. 42 Years
Answer :Option C

9X-7X=7

X=3.5

Therefore, Sachin’s Age is 7x3.5=24.5 Years

X+15=5(X-5)

x+10=4(x-5)

Q.5)

  1. 32 Years
  2. 40 Years
  3. 10 Years
  4. 42 Years
Answer :Option C

A=2+B......which is equal to 2+2X

B=2C......which is equal to 2X

C=X

Total of age= A+B+C=27

2+2X+2X+X=27

X=5

Now, B’s age =2X=10 Years
