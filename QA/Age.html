Ages

Age is defined as a period of time that a person has lived or a thing has existed. Age is measured in months, years, decades and so on. Problem based on age generally consists of information of ages of two or more persons and a relation between their ages in present/future/past. Using the information, it is asked to calculate the ages of one or more persons in present/future/past.ons divided by the number of observations.

Important Formulas on "Problems on Ages":

  • If the current age is x, then n times the age is nx.
  • If the current age is x, then age n years later/hence = x + n
  • If the current age is x, then age n years ago = x - n
  • The ages in a ratio a : b will be ax and bx.
