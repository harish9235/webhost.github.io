Q.1)
A,B,C and D divide a sum of money among themselves in the ratio of 7:4:3:2. If D gets Rs.500 less than A, Find total amount
  1. Rs.3500
  2. Rs.1600
  3. Rs.4300
  4. Rs.5500
Answer :Option B

Total of ratio is 7+4+3+2=16
If D gets Rs.500 less than A which is equal A-D ratio i. e 5 (Rs.500)
So for 1 ratio amount is Rs. 100
Therefore Total Amount is 16x100=Rs.1600

Q.2)
A bag contains 50p, 25p and 10p coins in the ratio 5:9:4, amounting to Rs.206.Find total no of coins in the bag
  1. 72 coins
  2. 40 coins
  3. 720 coins
  4. 32 coins
Answer :Option C

let ‘x’ will be number of coins
(0.50) (5x) + (0.25) (9x)+(0.10)(4x)=206
2.5x + 2.25x + 0.4x=206
X=206/5.15 Therefore x=40
So, Total Coins in the bag is = 5 (no.of 50p coins)x40 + 9 (no. of 25 coins)x40 + 4 (no. of 10p coins)x40 = 720 coins
