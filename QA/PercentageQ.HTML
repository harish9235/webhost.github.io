Practice Questions : Percentage
Q.1)
If A = 50 & B = 40 then, A is what % more than B?
  1. A is 10 % more than B
  2. A is 25 % more than B
  3. A is 15 % more than B
  4. A is 35 % more than B
Answer :Option B

consider 1% of B=40/100=0.4
A-B=50-40=10
Now, 10/0.4=25
Therefore, A is 25 % more than B.

Q.2)
A = 50 & B = 40, if A is increase by 10 % and B is increase by 30% then what is the total percentage change ?
  1. 16.20%
  2. 12.12%
  3. 18.88%
  4. 20.08%
Answer :Option C

Total(before increasing)=50+40=90
if A is increase by 10 %=50+((50/100)x10)=55
B is increase by 30%=40+((40/100)x30)=5
Total(after increasing)=55+52=107
Change in total=107-90=17
Therefore total percentage change=17/(90/100)=18.88%

Q.3)
If length of rectangle is increase by 20% and breadth is increase by 10% then what is percentage change in area of rectangle?
  1. 22%
  2. 32%
  3. 16%
  4. 20%
Answer :Option B

Consider, length of rectangle L=100 m and breadth B=100 m so, Area=LxB=10000 m2
Length of rectangle is increase by 20%=100+20%=120 m
breadth of rectangle is increase by 10%=100+10%=110 m 120
Area of rectangle after increment=LxB=120x110=13200 m2
Now, Increase in Area of rectangle=13200-10000=3200
So.....Increase in area (percentage)=3200/(10000/100)=32%

Q.4)
The population of the district of solapur is 1,00,000 the rate of increase is 10% per annum. Find the population at the start of 3rd year ?
  1. 1,40,000
  2. 1,32,000
  3. 2,11,000
  4. 1,21,000
Answer :Option D

The population at the start of 3rd year means Population of 2 years
So, First year population=1,00,000 +10%=1,10,000
Second year population=1,10,000 +10%=1,21,000
Therefore at start of third year the population will be 1,21,000

Q.5)
Which option is better, a successive discount of 40% and 30% or flat discount of 70% ?
  1. successive discount of 40% and 30% is better
  2. flat 70% is Better
  3. Both
  4. None of the above
Answer :Option B

Let’s Consider the amount is 100
Case A: successive discount of 40% and 30% ,it means first 40% discount on amount, after that 30% discount on remaining amount i.e 100-40%=60,
now 60-30%=42….so, final amount after 40% and 30% discount is 42
Case B: flat 70% discount i.e 100-70%=30…so, final amount after flat 70% is 30
Therefore Case B is better
