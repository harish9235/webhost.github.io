Practice Questions : Percentage
Q.1)
A man buys and an article for Rs. 100, and sells it for 110. Then the profit percent is ?
  1. 15%
  2. 10%
  3. 20%
  4. 30%
Answer :Option B

Cost price= Rs.100
Selling price= Rs.110
Profit=Selling price - Cost price=110-100=10
Profit percent=(profit x 100)/cost price
=(10 x 100)/100 = 10%
Therefore profit percent is 10%

  1. 15%
  2. 10%
  3. 20%
  4. 30%

Cost price=Rs.3 for 10 pencils so, per pencil=0.3
Sell price=Rs. 3 for 8 pencils so, Out of 10 he sold 8, so remaining pencils are 2
Gain=2 x per pencil price = Rs 0.6
Gain percent=(gain x 100)/cost price
=(0.6 x 100)/ 3
=20%
