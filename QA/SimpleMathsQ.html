Q.4)
Calculate the radius of a circle if its area is 25π m2
  1. 6 m
  2. 3 m
  3. 6 m
  4. 5 m
Answer :Option D

Area = 25π m2
We know that area of a circle = πr2
Hence, we can write,
25π = πr2
25 = r2
Hence, r = 5 m
Therefore, the radius of the circle is 5 m
