Blood Relations

Blood Relations is the most studied topic of Logical Reasoning and is one of the few topics which has found its importance in almost every entrance exam. Any relation in the world which either by birth or by marriage is called a Blood Relation

  • Any relation by birth will be mother, father, son, daughter, etc. and any relation by marriage will be father-in-law, mother-in-law, etc
  • From Below will get to know various blood Relations
    • Type of Relationship Terminology in Use
    Mother's or Father's son Myself/Sister
    Mother's or Father's daughter Myself/Sister
    Mother's or Father's brother Uncle
    Mother's or Father's sister Aunt
    Mother's or father's father Grandfather
    Mother's or father's mother Grandmother
    Son's wife Daughter-in-wife
    Daughter's husband son-in-law
    Husband's or wife's sister Sister-in-law
    Husband's or wife's brother Brother-in -law
    Brother's son Nephew
    Brother's daughter Niece
    Uncle or aunt's son or daughter Cousin

    Brother's wife Sister-in-law
    Grandson's or Granddaughter's daughter Great-granddaughter
