Alpha Numeric Series
  • Alphanumeric characters are made up of the 26 letters of the alphabet (A to Z) and the digits 0 to 9. So, 1, 2, q, f, m, p, and 10 are all examples of letters and numbers

  • Alphanumeric characters also include characters like *, &, and @. You can also use more than one of these characters together

  • Some examples of alphanumeric characters are &AF54hh, jjHF47, and @qw99O. These characters are made up of special symbols, numbers, and letters

  • You can write the letters of the alphabet in lowercase or uppercase. Whether or not case sensitivity is used depends on how it is being used
