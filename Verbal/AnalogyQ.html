Q.1)
EL : HJ :: DY : ?
  1. YT
  2. GW
  3. PL
  4. PP
Answer :Option B

E(5) + 3 = H(8) and L(12) – 2 = J(10)
Similarly, D(4) + 3 = G(7) and Y(25) – 2 = W(23)
Hence, GW will replace the question mark

Q.2)
RJ : IQ :: DH : ?
  1. YT
  2. GW
  3. WS
  4. PP
Answer :Option C

R – I (Reverse Letter of R is I) and J – Q (Reverse Letter of J is Q) Similarly, D – W (Reverse letter of D is W) and H – S (Reverse letter of H is S) Hence, WS will replace the question mark

Q.3)
Afghanistan : Kabul :: USA : ?
  1. Delhi
  2. Washington
  3. Mumbai
  4. Gaja
Answer :Option B

Kabul is the capital of Afghanistan.Similarly, Washington is the capital of the USA.Hence, Washington will replace the question mark.

Q.4)
Abode : Dwelling :: Blend : ?
  1. Intern
  2. Taste
  3. Doing
  4. Mix
Answer :Option D

Abode is the synonym of Dwelling. Similarly, Mix is the synonym of Blend. Hence, Mix will replace the answer

Q.5)
Conductor : Orchestra :: Captain : ?
  1. Car
  2. Ambulance
  3. Ship
  4. Hotel
Answer :Option C

Ship (The relationship is the leader and the entity they lead: a conductor leads an orchestra and a captain leads a ship.)
