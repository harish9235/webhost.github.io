Q.1)
A man pointing to a photograph says, 'The lady in the photograph is my nephew's maternal grandmother." How is the lady in the photograph related to the man's sister who has no other sister ?
  1. Sister-in-law
  2. Mother
  3. Mother-in-law
  4. Cousin
Answer :Option B

Clearly, the lady is the grandmother of man's sister's son i.e.. the mother of the mother of man's sister's son i.e., the mother of man's sister. So, the lady is man's mother

Q.2)
Pointing to a woman, a man said, "Her father is the only son of my father." How is the man related to the woman ?
  1. Father
  2. Brother-in-law
  3. Brother
  4. Son
Answer :Option C

The man said, "Her father is the only son of my father."Then the woman's father and the man's is the same=> The woman and the man are brother and sisters.Hence, the man is the brother of the woman

Q.3)
Introducing a man to her husband , a woman said "His brother's father is the only son of my grandfather". How is the woman related to this man?
  1. Sister-in-law
  2. Sister
  3. Aunt
  4. Daughter
Answer :Option B

Only son of woman's grandfather - woman's father; Man's brother's father - Man's father So, man's father is woman's father i.e woman is the man's sister

Q.5)
